About

Mental health issues are widespread in countries all over the world. There is a global shortage in human resources delivering mental health services, leaving people with mental issues untreated, and in turn, causing unhappiness and decreased enjoyment.

In India specifically, there is a social stigma associated with asking for mental help, and resources such as helplines and emergency support are hard to find and inaccessible. In such situations, a reliable and helpful application can be of great help, by helping users get an idea about their feelings and acting as an information hub for commonly asked questions.

To accomplish the same, this project aims at building a mental help chatbot that asks users to fill out an interactive questionnaire to determine and suggest whether professional help is recommended to be sought.

Furthermore, the chatbot will have a vast collection of answers to the most frequently asked questions regarding mental health. All of this will be presented in a user friendly graphical interface with an emphasis on usability and privacy.
